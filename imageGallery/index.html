MY FAV SERIES IN



Money Heist

The "Money Heist" (La Casa de Papel) series is a work of fiction and is not intended to depict real events. The show follows a group of criminals who plan and execute a heist on the Royal Mint of Spain.

Peaky Blinders

The Peaky Blinders were a street gang based in Birmingham, England, which operated from the 1880s until the 1910s. The group consisted largely of young criminals from lower- to middle-class backgrounds.

breaking bad

The story stemmed from the creative genius of Vince Gilligan, who told NPR in 2019 that he was “not sure where the idea for the show came from,” but that he remembers “the exact moment in idea hit” him.

Stranger Things

Stranger Things was inspired by the somewhat dubious Montauk Project. The Montauk Experiment was featured on a season 8 episode of Discovery Channel's Mysteries of the Abandoned on October 23, 2003.

Berlin

"Berlin" serves as both a spinoff and a prequel to Money Heist, showing the titular character's adventures before the events of the original series. It explores the backstory of Andres de Fonollosa.

Wednesday

Though a work of fiction, the script was inspired by the 11 July 2006 Mumbai train bombings. In fact, the incidents that followed the bombings were used as details in the plot.

Umbrella

This Netflix Original series is based on the comics of the same name by Gabriel Bá and Gerard Way. The first season of the show premiered in February 2019, and the most current season was released in June 2022.

Elite

It also shines light on the complexities that arise among the students, the bridge that gets created between the rich and the not so rich, and the constant fight to fit in with the popular kids.
